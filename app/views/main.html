Welcome to Zumbio!

We are Stockholm based StartUp specializing in low-cost 2D LiDAR enabled obstacle detection and avoidance for mobile robots, operating in inaccessible, rugged areas, such as underground mines. Our solution is unique in the market of industrial drone mounted sensor technology. With Zumbio! significant decreases in cost, as well as increases in operational safety, can be achieved (i.e. applicable for shaft blockage accidents and stope explorations after blasts).
